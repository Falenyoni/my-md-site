Nebuchadnezzar's dream in Daniel 2 is one of the most remarkable prophecies in Scripture — it traces world history from Babylon to the Second Coming with stunning accuracy.

## Outline

- Context: Nebuchadnezzar's forgotten dream
- The statue: head of gold, chest of silver, belly of bronze, legs of iron, feet of iron and clay
- The fulfillment:
  - Babylon (605–539 BC)
  - Medo-Persia (539–331 BC)
  - Greece (331–168 BC)
  - Rome (168 BC–476 AD)
  - The divided kingdoms of Europe (476 AD–present)
